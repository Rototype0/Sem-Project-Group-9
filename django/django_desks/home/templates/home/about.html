Why stand?

Studies show prolonged sitting can lead to back pain, obesity, and cardiovascular diseases, while too much standing may cause fatigue and discomfort. Alternating between sitting and standing during work significantly improves health and productivity.

By reducing strain on the lower back and neck, standing desks help minimize discomfort caused by prolonged sitting. They encourage better posture, boost blood flow, and reduce fatigue. Standing desks have also been linked to improved energy levels and reduced weight gain.
